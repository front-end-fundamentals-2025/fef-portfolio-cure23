About me

I'm a passionate designer and coder currently studying New Media Design at Jönköping University.

Originally from Italy, my journey into design and technology has been anything but linear. While I've always had a deep passion for art and creativity, I initially pursued a science-focused path, studying biomedical engineering before realizing that my true calling lies at the intersection of creativity and technology. Now, I'm excited to be honing my skills in graphic design, user experience, and web development.

This portfolio is a collection of my work and learning journey, where I combine my love for art with my growing technical expertise in areas like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and UX/UI design.
